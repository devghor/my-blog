## CBL - City Bank Limited Portal

An enterprise-grade banking portal designed for City Bank Limited, providing secure transaction management, account services, and customer relationship management.

### Key Features

- **Account Management**: Comprehensive account overview and management
- **Transaction Processing**: Real-time transaction handling with security protocols
- **Customer Portal**: Self-service portal for banking customers
- **Reporting Dashboard**: Advanced analytics and reporting for bank operations
- **Security**: Multi-factor authentication and encryption standards

### Technologies Used

- **Backend**: PHP Laravel
- **Frontend**: React.js
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **Security**: OAuth 2.0, JWT authentication

### Outcome

Enhanced customer experience with 24/7 banking services and reduced branch dependency by 45%.
