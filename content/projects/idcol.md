## IDCOL - Infrastructure Development Company Portal

A robust project management and financing platform for Infrastructure Development Company Limited (IDCOL), managing renewable energy and infrastructure projects.

### Key Features

- **Project Tracking**: End-to-end project lifecycle management
- **Financial Management**: Loan disbursement and tracking system
- **Document Management**: Centralized document repository with version control
- **Stakeholder Portal**: Multi-level access for different stakeholders
- **Reporting System**: Automated project and financial reports

### Technologies Used

- **Backend**: PHP Laravel
- **Frontend**: Vue.js
- **Database**: MySQL
- **Storage**: AWS S3 for document management

### Outcome

Streamlined project management for 200+ renewable energy projects with improved tracking efficiency.
