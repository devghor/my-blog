## FSD - Facility Service Desk

A comprehensive Facility Service Desk system designed to streamline facility management operations, maintenance requests, and service delivery across multiple locations.

### Key Features

- **Service Request Management**: Centralized system for submitting and tracking facility service requests
- **Work Order System**: Automated work order generation and assignment to technicians
- **Asset Management**: Complete tracking of facility assets, equipment, and inventory
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Scheduled maintenance planning and execution
- **Vendor Management**: Vendor registration, performance tracking, and contract management
- **Real-time Dashboard**: Live monitoring of service requests, work orders, and team performance
- **Mobile Access**: Technicians can access and update work orders from mobile devices
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Comprehensive reports on facility operations and service metrics

### Technologies Used

- **Backend**: PHP Laravel
- **Frontend**: Vue.js, Bootstrap
- **Database**: MySQL
- **API**: RESTful API architecture
- **Mobile**: Progressive Web App (PWA)

### Modules

1. **Service Request Module**
   - Multi-category service requests
   - Priority-based ticket system
   - Automated routing and assignment
   - Email notifications

2. **Work Order Management**
   - Work order lifecycle tracking
   - Technician assignment and scheduling
   - Time tracking and cost estimation
   - Material and spare parts management

3. **Asset Management**
   - Asset registry and QR code tagging
   - Maintenance history tracking
   - Warranty management
   - Depreciation calculation

4. **Preventive Maintenance**
   - Maintenance schedule planning
   - Automated work order generation
   - Equipment downtime tracking
   - Compliance checklists

5. **Vendor Management**
   - Vendor registration and rating
   - Contract management
   - Service level agreement (SLA) tracking
   - Payment processing

### Outcome

Successfully deployed across multiple corporate facilities, managing 5000+ service requests monthly with improved response time by 50% and reduced operational costs by 35%.
